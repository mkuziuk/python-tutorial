SYNTHETIC DERIVATIVE DATA NOTICE

The underlying Digits images are loaded from scikit-learn's copy of the UCI
Optical Recognition of Handwritten Digits dataset. The UCI record identifies
that dataset as CC BY 4.0. Attribution: E. Alpaydin and C. Kaynak, Optical
Recognition of Handwritten Digits, UCI Machine Learning Repository, 1998,
DOI 10.24432/C50P49.

The new metadata, source fingerprints, noise transforms and scanner-C stress
transform are instructional synthetic modifications created for this tutorial.
They are offered under CC BY 4.0 with attribution to “Python-расследования”.
